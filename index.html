How it works?

Prepare a device and connect it to Dashy

Install our script and be ready to claim the device

Claim the device on your admin panel

Authenticate with your Google account

Add a new device and claim your dashboard

Setup your dashboard

Change Name, Urls and Interval

Enjoy your dashboards on your device
